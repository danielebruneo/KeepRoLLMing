# PROPOSE-CATALYST-CORE-CHANGE

## Goal
Take a local adaptation or repeated lesson and turn it into a structured proposal for the CATALYST core.

## Procedure
1. Identify the local adaptation, overlay, or lesson.
2. Explain the repeated friction it solves.
3. State why it appears generalizable beyond this project.
4. Record the proposal in `_agent/self/CORE_CHANGE_CANDIDATES.md`.
5. If mature enough, prepare `PROPOSE-CATALYST-PULL-REQUEST`.
